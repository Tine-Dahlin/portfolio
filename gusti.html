Gusti Leader

1.semester exam - individuel project

· Developed with HTML5 and CSS3
· Responsive website for desktop, tablet and mobile
· Moodboard, sketches, wireframes, mockups, logo
· Logo, icons and illustrations designed in Adobe illustrator
· Mockups and wireframes designed in Figma

'Gusti Leather' is a website that sells sophisticated vintagedesign products combined with sustainable business and transparency. All their products are made of real leather. Based on the exam description, I have chosen to create a reliable information page about 'Gusti leather' that can inform about their sustainability.

I focused on creating a universe where customers can buy leather with good conscience and at the same time increase the company's marketing with increased sales. Based on the wishes of the target group, I illustrated the information with the use of drawings and icons to create a different and fun universe.
