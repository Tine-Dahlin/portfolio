Tusindfryd

2.semester exam - group project

· Developed with HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript
· Responsive website for desktop, tablet and mobile
· Cover and product pictures taken
· Moodboard, sketches, wireframes, mockups, styletile
· Icons and logo designed in Adobe Illustrator
· Designmanual
· SoMe strategy and visuel content

Tusindfryd is a small flower shop located in Jels. The shop needed help optimizing its digital platforms. The purpose of the project was to strengthen the brand and increase the business awareness.

We created a new website for the customer with a focus on user experience, image optimization and a design manual for the customer. We also developed a SoMe strategy and visual content for different platforms, to increase the business' awareness.
